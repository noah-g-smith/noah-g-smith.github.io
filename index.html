Noah's Story

Noah graduated from Monash University in 2019 with a Bachelor of Science majoring in astrophysics and minoring in mathematics. Astrophysics at Monash provided an introduction to scientific programming which took the form of programming astrophysical models and working with large data sets.

Since then Noah has set his focus on programming daily, learning data science relevant concepts, and creating data science projects. In contrast to others entering the field as an IT graduate, a heavy background in mathematics has provided extremely useful. For many tackling computer science problems, the underlying mathematics is the crux of the problem.

An almost obsessive interest in problem solving and mathematics has kept Noah continuously learning on his own time. Whenever a real world situation poses a problem he is drawn to think, “What mathematics describes this situation and can I solve it?”
