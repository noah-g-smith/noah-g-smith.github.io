Noah's Story

In 2019 I received a Bachelor of Science from Monash University with a major in astrophysics and minor in mathematics. Astrophysics at Monash provided an introduction to scientific programming in the form of developing astrophysical (computational) models and working with large data sets. The mathematics provided a solid foundation for this work, the confidence to tackle challenging new problems, and the impetus to do it with rigour.

Since graduation I have set my focus on developing the skillset for a career in data science: leaning the relevant foundational concepts, solving real-world problems, programming daily, and, more recently, building a portfolio to demonstrate my competencies. If there is an underlying motivation for my work, it is a passion for problem solving, logic, and mathematics. I am drawn to frame problem spaces in mathematical terms - 'What mathematics describes this situation, this problem, and potential solutions'. And data science is a field where I can put this passion to work..
